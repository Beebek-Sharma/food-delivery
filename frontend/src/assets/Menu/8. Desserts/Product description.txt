 Dessert 

1. Gulab Jamun 
   Soft and syrupy, our Gulab Jamun features milk-based dumplings soaked in rose-flavored syrup, offering a rich, sweet delight.

2. Rasgulla 
   Light and spongy, our Rasgulla is made from soft, cottage cheese balls soaked in sugar syrup, delivering a refreshing sweetness.

3. Gajar ka Halwa 
   Our Gajar ka Halwa is a comforting dessert made with grated carrots, milk, and ghee, cooked to perfection with a touch of sweetness.

4. Ras Malai 
   Delicate and creamy, our Ras Malai consists of soft cheese dumplings soaked in sweet, cardamom-flavored milk for a luxurious taste.

5. Jalebi with Rabri 
   Crispy, spiral-shaped jalebis paired with creamy rabri, our Jalebi with Rabri is a delicious combination of textures and flavors.

6. Kulfi Falooda 
   Our Kulfi Falooda is a delightful dessert of creamy, traditional kulfi topped with falooda noodles and sweet syrup, perfect for a refreshing treat.

7. Kheer
   A creamy rice pudding, our Kheer is flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts, offering a sweet, comforting dessert.

8. Malpua 
   Golden and crispy, our Malpua is a deep-fried pancake soaked in syrup, offering a unique combination of sweetness and texture.

9. Moong Dal Halwa  
   Rich and aromatic, our Moong Dal Halwa is a warm dessert made with ground lentils, ghee, and sugar, for a melt-in-your-mouth experience.

10. Kesari Phirni  
    Our Kesari Phirni is a creamy, saffron-flavored rice pudding, delicately sweet and served chilled for a delightful finish.
