 Breads 

1. Butter Naan  
   Our Butter Naan is a soft, fluffy flatbread brushed with melted butter, making it a perfect companion for rich curries.

2. Garlic Naan 
   Flavored with fresh garlic and butter, our Garlic Naan is a tasty and aromatic flatbread that enhances any meal.

3. Plain Roti 
   A simple, wholesome flatbread, our Plain Roti is made from whole wheat flour and pairs well with any dish.

4. Tandoori Roti  
   Our Tandoori Roti is a crispy, flavorful flatbread cooked in a tandoor, adding a smoky taste to each bite.

5. Aloo Paratha
   Stuffed with seasoned potatoes, our Aloo Paratha is a hearty, spiced flatbread, perfect for a filling meal.

6. Lachha Paratha  
   A layered, flaky bread, our Lachha Paratha is made with whole wheat and cooked until golden and crisp.

7. Missi Roti 
   Our Missi Roti is a traditional flatbread made with gram flour and spices, providing a flavorful, rustic taste.

8. Cheese Naan 
   Filled with melted cheese, our Cheese Naan is a soft, indulgent flatbread with a deliciously creamy center.

9. Pudina Paratha  
   Flavored with fresh mint, our Pudina Paratha is a refreshing, flaky bread that adds a hint of freshness to any meal.

10. Kashmiri Naan 
    Our Kashmiri Naan is a mildly sweet, stuffed flatbread with dried fruits and nuts, offering a delightful, unique flavor.
