  Salads

1. Kachumber Salad
   Our Kachumber Salad is a refreshing mix of chopped cucumbers, tomatoes, and onions, tossed with tangy spices for a burst of freshness in every bite.

2. Sprouts Salad
   A protein-rich mix, our Sprouts Salad combines fresh sprouts with vegetables, lightly seasoned for a crunchy, nutritious experience.

3. Green Salad with Mint Dressing 
   Crisp greens drizzled with a refreshing mint dressing, our Green Salad is light and zesty, perfect for a fresh start to any meal.

4. Paneer Tikka Salad 
   Our Paneer Tikka Salad features soft paneer tikka cubes on a bed of fresh greens, offering a tasty blend of smoky and refreshing flavors.

5. Chana Chaat 
   A zesty blend of chickpeas, veggies, and spices, our Chana Chaat is a flavorful and nutritious salad with a tangy kick.

6. Greek Salad with Indian Twist  
   Our Greek Salad with an Indian Twist combines fresh vegetables, olives, and feta with a hint of Indian spices, offering a unique flavor fusion.

7. Chicken Tikka Caesar Salad 
   A hearty twist on the classic Caesar, our Chicken Tikka Caesar Salad combines smoky chicken tikka with fresh greens and creamy dressing.

8. Quinoa Salad with Pomegranate  
   Nutty quinoa meets juicy pomegranate in our Quinoa Salad, providing a delightful mix of texture, flavor, and nutrition in every bite.

9. Aloo Chaat Salad
   Our Aloo Chaat Salad features spiced potato cubes with tangy chutneys and veggies, creating a vibrant and delicious chaat-style salad.

10. Tandoori Veggie Salad 
    Fresh veggies marinated in tandoori spices and grilled to perfection, our Tandoori Veggie Salad delivers a smoky, flavorful bite every time.
