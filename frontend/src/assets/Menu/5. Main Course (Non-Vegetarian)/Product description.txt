1. Butter Chicken 
   Our Butter Chicken features tender chicken pieces in a creamy, mildly spiced tomato-based gravy, offering a rich and comforting taste.

2. Chicken Tikka Masala 
   Marinated chicken tikka in a spiced, creamy tomato sauce, our Chicken Tikka Masala delivers a bold, smoky flavor in every bite.

3. Mutton Rogan Josh 
   Our Mutton Rogan Josh is a slow-cooked, aromatic mutton curry with a blend of Kashmiri spices, resulting in a rich, flavorful dish.

4. Fish Curry

5. Prawn Malai Curry

6. Chicken Chettinad 
   Our Chicken Chettinad is a fiery South Indian dish with bold spices and coconut, providing a deeply aromatic and spicy flavor.

7. Lamb Vindaloo

8. Keema Matar

9. Egg Curry 
   Our Egg Curry features boiled eggs in a spiced tomato and onion gravy, creating a comforting and mildly spiced dish.

10. Fish Amritsari Masala
