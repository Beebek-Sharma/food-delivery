   Main Course (Vegetarian)


1. Paneer Butter Masala  
   Our Paneer Butter Masala features soft paneer in a creamy, mildly spiced tomato gravy, delivering a rich and comforting flavor.

2. Palak Paneer  
   A nourishing blend of spinach and paneer, our Palak Paneer is seasoned with delicate spices for a creamy and flavorful experience.

3. Dal Makhani 
   Our Dal Makhani is a slow-cooked mix of black lentils and kidney beans in a buttery, mildly spiced gravy, making it a comforting classic.

4. Veg Kolhapuri
   A spicy mix of vegetables, our Veg Kolhapuri is cooked in a flavorful Kolhapuri masala, offering a vibrant and robust taste.

5. Aloo Gobhi Masala 
   Our Aloo Gobhi Masala is a hearty dish of potatoes and cauliflower, perfectly spiced and cooked to a tender, flavorful finish.

6. Baingan Bharta
   Smoky and flavorful, our Baingan Bharta combines roasted eggplant with spices and herbs, creating a deliciously unique taste.

7. Malai Kofta 
   Our Malai Kofta features soft, creamy dumplings in a rich, mildly spiced gravy, offering a melt-in-the-mouth experience.

8. Chole Masala
   A popular North Indian classic, our Chole Masala is a spicy and tangy chickpea curry, simmered with a blend of aromatic spices.

9. Dum Aloo 
   Baby potatoes cooked in a rich, spiced gravy, our Dum Aloo offers a creamy, flavorful experience with every bite.

10. Bhindi Masala 
    Our Bhindi Masala is a tasty combination of fresh okra and spices, stir-fried to perfection for a flavorful, satisfying dish.
