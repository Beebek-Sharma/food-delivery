 Rice and biryani dish 

1. Veg Biryani 
   Our Veg Biryani is a flavorful mix of basmati rice and vegetables, cooked with fragrant spices for a delicious, aromatic experience.

2. Chicken Biryani
   A classic favorite, our Chicken Biryani features tender chicken pieces layered with spiced rice, delivering a rich and savory taste.

3. Mutton Biryani  
   Our Mutton Biryani is a hearty dish of basmati rice and tender mutton, slow-cooked with spices for a bold and flavorful meal.

4. Jeera Rice 
   Simple and aromatic, our Jeera Rice is made with fluffy basmati rice and tempered with cumin seeds, adding a subtle flavor to any meal.

5. Ghee Rice
   Fragrant and comforting, our Ghee Rice is cooked with pure ghee and delicate spices, offering a buttery and rich taste.

6. Prawn Biryani  
   Juicy prawns cooked with spiced basmati rice, our Prawn Biryani combines seafood flavors with aromatic spices in every bite.

7. Peas Pulao
   A light and mildly spiced rice dish, our Peas Pulao features tender peas and basmati rice, providing a subtle, fresh taste.

8. Hyderabadi Biryani (Dum)  
   Our Hyderabadi Biryani is a traditional dum-cooked biryani with layers of spiced meat and rice, delivering a rich, flavorful experience.

9. Saffron Rice 
   Delicately flavored with saffron, our Saffron Rice is a fragrant and mildly sweet dish, perfect as a side or standalone.

10. Egg Fried Rice 
    A savory and satisfying dish, our Egg Fried Rice combines fluffy rice and scrambled eggs with a hint of spices for a tasty meal.
