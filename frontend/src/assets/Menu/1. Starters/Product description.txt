  Starters 


1. Paneer Tikka  
   Our Paneer Tikka is a flavorful treat of soft paneer cubes, marinated in aromatic spices, and perfectly grilled for a smoky, delicious experience.

2. Hara Bhara Kebab  
   A hearty mix of spinach and green peas, our Hara Bhara Kebab is packed with nutrients and spiced just right for a tasty, guilt-free snack.

3. Aloo Tikki Chaat  
   Our Aloo Tikki Chaat offers a delightful blend of crispy potato patties topped with tangy yogurt, chutneys, and crunchy sev for a vibrant, zesty bite.

4. Samosa Chaat  
   Crispy samosas combined with a medley of chutneys, yogurt, and spices, our Samosa Chaat delivers a perfect mix of textures and flavors in each bite.

5. Tandoori Mushrooms
   Marinated in a flavorful spice blend, our Tandoori Mushrooms are grilled to perfection, delivering a smoky, juicy taste in every mouthful.

6. Veg Spring Rolls 
   Crisp and light, our Veg Spring Rolls are packed with fresh vegetables and lightly spiced, offering a delightful, crunchy snack with every bite.

7. Chicken Malai Tikka  
   Our Chicken Malai Tikka features tender, creamy chicken pieces marinated in mild spices and grilled for a juicy, melt-in-your-mouth experience.

8. Fish Amritsari
   Batter-fried to a golden crisp, our Fish Amritsari is marinated with special spices, giving each piece a flavorful and satisfying crunch.

9. Seekh Kebab 
   Minced meat seasoned with aromatic spices, our Seekh Kebab is grilled to perfection, offering a smoky, juicy bite in each skewer.

10. Mutton Shami Kebab 
    Delicate and flavorful, our Mutton Shami Kebab is made with finely minced mutton and spices, cooked until tender for a rich, satisfying experience.
